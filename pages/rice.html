What is it?

Rice grains that have been brought to a boil and left to absorb water until it expands and turns fluffy. Rice is a staple food of many dishes and while good on its own, is used as a complement to other forms of meats and vegetables.

Ingredients and equipment

  • Jasmine rice, 250g.
  • Water, 750mL.
  • Rice cooker or stovetop.

Recipe - Using a rice cooker

  1. Wash rice in a pot with water until water is only slightly cloudy.
  2. To the rice cooker, add rice and water. Ideally, the level of water should be at least an inch above the level of the rice.
  3. Set rice cooker to desired setting and wait the specified time.
  4. Rice can be used in other dishes containing meats and vegetables or served as is with soy sauce.

Recipe - Using a pot

  1. Wash rice a pot with water until water is only slightly cloudy.
  2. To a pot, add rice and water. The level of the water should be at least an inch above the level of the rice.
  3. Turn on the heat and bring the water to a boil.
  4. Once the water starts to bubble frequently, i.e. bubbles start popping audibly, immediately turn off the heat and cover the pot with a lid.
  5. Let sit for 10 minutes or until the excess water is absorbed by the rice.
  6. Break up the rice using a fork.
