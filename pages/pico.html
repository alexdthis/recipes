What is it?

Pico de Gallo is a dish that contains tomatoes, cilantro, onions, and lime juice. This dish is commonly confused with salsa. This dish is used as a complement to various other dishes like as a condiment in burritos, rice dishes, and tacos.

Ingredients and equipment

  • Tomatos, diced, six.
  • Cilantro, diced, two bundles.
  • Onions, diced, three.
  • Lime juice, squeezed from eight limes.

Recipe

  1. To a mixing bowl or large pan, add in all ingredients.
  2. Mix thoroughly and place in fridge for two to three hours.
  3. After taking out of fridge, can be used in dishes as condiments or complements.
